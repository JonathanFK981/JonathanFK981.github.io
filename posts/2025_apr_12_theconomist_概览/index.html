The world this week.

Donald Trump pulled back from his latest and biggest round of punitive tariffs by countries that did not impose announcing a 90-day pause for them down to a 10% tariff. The retaliatory trade duties, taking American president said more to negotiate. He did not offer than 75 countries were willing any relief for China, however, instead raising the tariff rate on Chinese imports. China had earlier vowed to “fight to the end “as it imposed counter tariffs on American imports. It described the extra duties as “a mistake on top of a mistake.

唐纳德·特朗普在宣布对未实施报复性关税的国家暂缓90天征收最新且最大规模的惩罚性关税后，将其降至10%的关税。美国表示还有更多内容需要谈判，但并未提供具体细节。然而，特朗普并未对中国提供任何缓解措施，而是提高了中国进口商品的关税税率。中国早些时候誓言要“战斗到底”，并对美国进口商品实施了反制关税。中国称这些额外关税是“错上加错”。

Mr Trump and his defence secretary, Pete Hegseth, suggested that America’s defence budget for the next fiscal year will top $1trn for the first time when it is published next month. Congress authorised around $892bn for this year.

特朗普先生和他的国防部长皮特·赫格塞思建议，美国下一财政年度的国防预算在下个月公布时将首次超过1万亿美元。而国会为本年度授权的国防预算是大约8920亿美元。

Rasputin in the White House

Mr Trump sacked General Timothy Haugh as director of the National Security Agency. General Haugh was reportedly ousted by Mr Trump on the advice of Laura Loomer, a 31-year-old conspiracy theorist who has been banned from most social-media sites. She is still on X, where she wrote that General Haugh had been disloyal to Mr Trump. She provided no evidence.

“拉斯普京在白宫”

(这个短语可能是在用一种比喻的方式将某个具有类似于格里高利·拉斯普京（Grigori Rasputin）影响力或行为的人与白宫联系起来。拉斯普京是俄罗斯历史上一位极具争议的人物，以其对沙皇尼古拉二世及其家族的强大影响力而闻名。如果用在现代语境中，可能是暗示某人拥有过度的权力、操控能力或神秘的影响力。) 根据您提供的信息，特朗普先生解雇了国家安全局局长蒂莫西·豪少将。据报道，特朗普是在31岁的阴谋论者劳拉·卢默的建议下做出这一决定的，劳拉·卢默因传播阴谋论而被大多数社交媒体平台禁言，但她仍然活跃在X平台上，并在上面撰文称，豪少将对特朗普不忠，不过她并未提供任何证据支持她的说法。

Mr Hegseth became the first American defence secretary in decades to visit the Panama Canal, where he reiterated America’s intention to wrest the waterway away from what he described as Chinese influence. A deal to sell the canal’s ports operated by Ck Hutchison, a company in Hong Kong, to BlackRock, an American investment firm, looks increasingly shaky; the Trump-friendly Panamanian government is opening an investigation into CK Hutchison’s port contracts.

赫格塞思成为几十年来首位访问巴拿马运河的美国国防部长。他在访问期间重申了美国打算将这条水道从他所描述的“中国影响力”中夺回的意图。此前，香港公司长江和记实业（CK Hutchison）计划将其运营的运河港口出售给美国投资公司贝莱德（BlackRock），但这项交易如今看起来愈发岌岌可危；巴拿马亲特朗普政府已开始对长江和记的港口合同展开调查。

The legal battle over deporting alleged Venezuelan gang members to EI Salvador took another twist. The Supreme Court decided that the deportations could continue, but that the migrants could challenge their removal in the legal jurisdictions where they are being held. That caused two judges in New York and Texas to delay the deportation of migrants until they received a hearing.

关于将涉嫌的委内瑞拉帮派成员驱逐到萨尔瓦多的法律斗争出现了新的转折。最高法院裁定，驱逐程序可以继续进行，但移民可以在他们被关押的司法辖区挑战其被驱逐出境的决定。这一裁决导致纽约和德克萨斯的两位法官暂时停止了对移民的驱逐行动，直到他们能够获得听证会的机会。这表明，在执行驱逐前提供法律听证的权利成为了这些案件中的一个重要考量因素。

The IMF announced a preliminary deal with Argentina fora funding programme worth $20bn. President Javier Milei, who has radically cut spending and reduced inflation, celebrated the announcement. It is expected to herald the beginning of a difficult but much needed shift to a more flexible exchange rate.

国际货币基金组织（IMF）宣布与阿根廷达成了一项初步协议，提供一项价值200亿美元的资助计划。阿根廷总统哈维尔·米莱伊庆祝了这一宣布，他已大幅削减开支并降低了通货膨胀。预计这将标志着向更加灵活的汇率机制转变的开始，尽管这一过程将是艰难的，但对阿根廷来说是非常必要的。

The death toll from the collapse of a roof at a nightclub in the Dominican Republic rose to almost 200. The club was hosting a concert by Rubby Perez, a popular singer, who was killed. Politicians, sportsmen and celebrities were also at the venue.

多米尼加共和国一家夜总会发生的屋顶坍塌事故导致的死亡人数已上升至近200人。事发时，该夜总会正在举办由著名歌手鲁比·佩雷斯（Rubby Perez）表演的音乐会，而鲁比·佩雷斯本人也不幸遇难。据报道，当时场内还有政客、运动员和名人等在场。这起悲剧事件引起了广泛的关注和哀悼。

During a visit by Binyamin Netanyahu, the Israeli prime minister, to the White House Donald Trump announced that America would start “direct” talks with Iran about its nuclear programme. Mr Netanyahu had hoped to persuade the American president that it was time instead to bomb Iran’s nuclear sites.

在以色列总理本雅明·内塔尼亚胡访问白宫期间，唐纳德·特朗普总统宣布美国将开始与伊朗就其核计划进行“直接”谈判。而内塔尼亚胡原本希望说服美国总统，认为现在是时候轰炸伊朗的核设施了，而不是通过谈判解决。

A video was published of Israeli troops killing 15 Palestinian medics near Rafah last month. The Israeli army claimed the medics’ convoy was driving suspiciously, without lights or sirens. But the footage, obtained from the phone of a dead medic, showed that the ambulances had their lights and signals on. The Israeli army now says the episode is “under thorough examination”.

一段视频近日曝光，显示以色列军队上个月在拉法附近杀害了15名巴勒斯坦医护人员。以色列军方声称，这些医护人员的车队行驶方式可疑，没有开灯或鸣响警报器。然而，从一名已故医护人员的手机中获取的视频画面显示，救护车当时确实开着灯光并发出信号。以色列军方现在表示，这一事件正在“进行全面调查”。

Israel struck a number of military targets in Syria, escalating tensions with Turkey. Relations between the two regional powers have worsened since the war in Gaza began. They are now competing for influence in Syria.

以色列对叙利亚的多个军事目标实施了打击，这一行动加剧了与土耳其之间的紧张关系。自从加沙战争开始以来，这两个地区大国的关系就已经恶化。目前，它们在叙利亚的竞争进一步升级，双方都在寻求扩大在该地区的影响力。

Amnesty International recorded more than 1,500 executions around the world in 2024, the highest level fora decade. Iran, Iraq and Saudi Arabia accounted for 1,380 of them. The figures do not cover a number of countries, including China, believed to be the world’s biggest executioner.

国际特赦组织（Amnesty International）记录显示，2024年全球范围内有超过1500人被执行死刑，这是十年来的最高水平。其中，伊朗、伊拉克和沙特阿拉伯三国共计占1380例。这些数字并未涵盖一些国家，包括中国，据信中国是世界上执行死刑最多的国家。

Planned peace talks between a rebel group backed by Congo’s government and M23, Rwanda, were postponed indefinitely, shattering the latest hope of easing the brutal conflict in eastern Congo. Thousands of people have been killed and tens of thousands displaced in the fighting.

由刚果政府支持的一支叛军组织与M23（卢旺达支持的武装组织）之间的计划和谈被无限期推迟，这粉碎了缓解刚果东部残酷冲突的最新希望。这场冲突已导致数千人死亡，数万人流离失所。

Nigeria received 1m doses of vaccine to help contain a meningitis outbreak that has probably many more, in the killed at least 150 people, and poor north. The country suffers regular outbreaks of the disease which its underfunded health-care system struggles to control. Friedrich Merz, Germany’s all-but-guaranteed next chancellor, unveiled a coalition deal between his Christian Democrats and the Social Democrats (SPD). If SPD members approve the agreement Mr Merz should be sworn into office in May. His popularity has fallen since he decided to increase state borrowing, after campaigning against doing so.

尼日利亚收到了100万剂疫苗，以帮助遏制一场脑膜炎疫情的蔓延。这场疫情可能已经导致至少150人死亡，主要集中在贫困的北部地区。由于医疗系统资金不足，该国经常遭受此类疾病的爆发，控制疫情的能力受到严重限制。与此同时，德国几乎已锁定的下一任总理弗里德里希·默茨（Friedrich Merz）公布了一项由他的基督教民主联盟（CDU）与社会民主党（SPD）达成的联合政府协议。如果社民党成员批准该协议，默茨预计将在5月宣誓就职。然而，由于他在竞选期间反对增加国家借贷，却在当选后决定这样做，其支持率已经有所下降。

Volodymyr Zelensky said that Ukraine had captured two Chinese nationals fighting for Russia in eastern Ukraine and that at least 155 others were on the battlefield. China’s foreign ministry said it was verifying the claims, and that it does not support Chinese nationals’ involvement “in any party’s military operations” North Korea has sent troops to aid Russia, but they fought in Russia’s Kursk region, not on Ukrainian territory. Ukraine has been mostly pushed out of Kursk, though Mr Zelensky has confirmed that his forces are now fighting in Belgorod.

乌克兰总统泽连斯基表示，乌克兰在东部地区抓获了两名为中国国籍、为俄罗斯作战的人员，并称至少还有155名中国公民在战场上参与战斗。中国外交部回应称正在核实相关说法，并强调不支持中国公民“参与任何一方的军事行动”。与此同时，朝鲜已向俄罗斯派遣军队以提供援助，但这些部队主要在俄罗斯的库尔斯克地区作战，而非乌克兰领土。尽管乌克兰部队此前被逐出库尔斯克地区，但泽连斯基确认，乌军目前正在与俄罗斯接壤的别尔哥罗德（Belgorod）地区展开战斗。

The British government dropped plans for official inquiries in five towns where sex grooming gangs had abused young girls. The inquiries had been announced in January amid a furore over the gangs, which largely consisted of perpetrators from Pakistan or with Pakistani heritage. The government will instead make funds available to local authorities and allow them to decide where independent local inquiries are appropriate. The decision was met by claims that the victims of abuse had been betrayed.

英国政府放弃了对五个城镇中涉及性侵年轻女孩的性侵团伙进行官方调查的计划。这些调查原本是在一月份宣布的，当时公众对这些主要由巴基斯坦裔或有巴基斯坦背景的人组成的性侵团伙感到愤怒。现在，政府决定向地方当局提供资金，并允许他们自行决定是否在适当的情况下开展独立的地方调查。这一决定引发了批评，认为这是对受害者的背叛。

Goodbye, Mr Yoon

South Korea is to hold a presidential election on June 3rd. The announcement came after the Constitutional Court removed Yoon Suk Yeol from office, following his impeachment by the legislature in mid-December. Mr Yoon had briefly imposed martial law in early December, a political shock that revived memories of the country’s military dictatorships from the 1960s to the 1980s. All eight judges on the court voted to remove Mr Yoon, sharply criticising him for violating “the principles of the rule of law and democracy”.

“再见，尹先生” 韩国总统尹锡悦（Yoon Suk-yeol） 韩国将于6月3日举行总统选举。这一决定是在宪法法院于12月中旬通过弹劾案后，正式解除尹锡悦（Yoon Suk-yeol）总统职务的背景下宣布的。尹锡悦曾在12月初短暂实施戒严令，这一政治举动震惊全国，唤起了人们对韩国上世纪60年代至80年代军政府独裁统治的记忆。宪法法院的八名法官一致投票支持罢免尹锡悦，并严厉批评他违反了“法治和民主原则”。
